Welcome!

Our sustainable garden combines architectural innovation with artistic design. Explore unique ideas and eco-friendly creations.

Ecological Architecture

Ecological architecture aims to minimize the negative impact on the environment. Key principles include energy efficiency, water conservation, and sustainable materials.

Green Roof

Vegetation on rooftops improves insulation, biodiversity, and air quality.

Passive Solar Houses

Buildings designed to maximize sunlight and warmth, reducing energy consumption.

Rainwater Harvesting

Collect and reuse rainwater to conserve natural resources.

Our Green House

Our greenhouse is built from wood and recycled plastic bottles. The transparent roof provides sunlight while sheltering plants from rain.

Artistic Aspects

Our garden integrates art into nature, combining eco-friendly materials with creative designs that blend seamlessly into the environment.

Recycled Art

Decorative installations made from recycled materials, promoting sustainability and creativity.

Natural Patterns

Incorporate patterns inspired by nature in garden beds or pathways.

Natural Lighting

Natural light creates beautiful spaces in our garden.

How is the artistic aspects sustainable?

Our garden is sustainable because we use a lot of recicled material and we recicle materials like water bottles and bottle taps, reducing waste.

Materials that we use:

Water bottles

In our garden we do auto-irrigation vases made with water bottles.

Water pipes

In our garden we use water pipes to water plants.

Certified wood

We use certified wood to ensure any tree are in harm in this process.
